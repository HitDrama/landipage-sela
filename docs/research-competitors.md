## 3. Effectiveness Analysis
### Effective Features:
*   **Social Proof:** Displaying actual certificates and video testimonials builds immediate trust.
*   **Transparency:** Detailed breakdowns of what happens in each level/module.
*   **Convenience:** Quick registration forms (Name, Phone, Target Score).
*   **Authority Signals:** Badges from British Council, IDP, or Ministry of Education.

### Ineffective Features:
*   **Information Overload:** Too much text explaining "Why IELTS" instead of "Why us".
*   **Mobile Unfriendliness:** Heavy tables of course schedules that break on small screens.
*   **Vague CTAs:** Using "Learn More" instead of action-oriented "Get Free Consultation".

## 4. Cultural Considerations for Vietnamese Parents
*   **Prestige & Credentials:** Degrees and scores matter more than "fun" learning.
*   **Safe Investment:** Emphasis on "Cam káº¿t" (Commitment/Guarantee) reduces perceived risk.

*   **Zalo/Direct Contact:** Parents prefer chatting or calling over reading long FAQs.
