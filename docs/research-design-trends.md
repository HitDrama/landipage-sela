# EdTech Landing Page Design Trends 2025

## 1. Visual Trends
- **Bento Grids**: Essential for modular content; organizes features, testimonials, and stats into a clean, scannable grid (Ref: Apple, Linear styles).
- **Glassmorphism & Depth**: Using semi-transparent cards with background blurs to create hierarchy without clutter.
- **Neo-Minimalism**: High "breathing" space combined with bold accents.
- **3D & Interactive Assets**: Moving away from stock photos to 3D illustrations or product-in-action visuals.

## 2. Animation Patterns
- **Scroll-Based Storytelling**: Elements reveal or transform as the user scrolls to maintain engagement.
- **Micro-interactions**: Hover effects on CTAs and subtle feedback loops for form fields to reduce friction.
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Using animations to reveal details only when needed, keeping the initial view clean.

## 3. Typography for Vietnamese Market
- **Serif Comeback**: Elegant serifs (e.g., Playfair Display, Merriweather) for headings to evoke authority and tradition.
- **Modern Sans-Serifs**: Robust fonts (e.g., Be Vietnam Pro, Montserrat) that handle Vietnamese diacritics without visual "slop."
- **Bold Hierarchy**: Oversized, high-contrast headings for immediate value proposition impact.

## 4. Color Psychology for Education
- **Trust (Blue/Navy)**: Foundations of stability and reliability. Essential for institutional EdTech.
- **Energy & Warmth (Orange/Yellow)**: Stimulates creativity and optimism; perfect for K-12 or creative learning platforms.
- **Growth (Green)**: Symbolizes progress and new beginnings.
- **Sophisticated Accents**: Pairing traditional "education blue" with vibrant purples or teals to signal innovation.

## 5. Conversion-Focused Layouts
- **The 5-Second Hero**: Headline, benefit-driven sub-copy, and a clear CTA above the fold.
- **Social Proof Integration**: Logos of partners/schools and student testimonials placed early to build credibility.
- **Segmented Pathways**: Dedicated sections or buttons for different personas (e.g., "For Students" vs. "For Parents").
- **Multi-step Forms**: Breaking down registration into bite-sized steps with progress bars to boost completion rates.
- **Mobile-First Indexing**: Layouts designed for vertical scanning and thumb-friendly interactions.

## Unresolved Questions
- Specific performance benchmarks for glassmorphism-heavy designs on low-end mobile devices common in Vietnam.
- Long-term conversion impact of gamified CTA buttons versus traditional solid-color buttons.
